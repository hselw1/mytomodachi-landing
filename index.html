Why it works

Designed for real
conversation practice

Most apps teach vocabulary in isolation. My Tomodachi puts you in a real conversation — any time, any place.

🎭
Six real characters

Yuki in Osaka, Kenji the Tokyo salaryman, Hiroshi the Hokkaido fisherman. Each has a life, a dialect, and a personality — and they remember yours.

📊
JLPT level matching

Set N5 through N2 and the characters respond at exactly that level — vocabulary, kanji, grammar. No guessing at words you haven't learned yet.

あ
Furigana on demand

One tap shows furigana above every kanji. Another tap hides it. Study with support, or test yourself without — your choice every message.

🔊
Native-quality audio

Every response is spoken aloud using Google Neural TTS voices. Hear the actual rhythm and intonation of natural Japanese, not robotic synthesis.

🌐
Instant translation

Don't understand a sentence? Tap translate for the full English meaning. Tap individual sentences to check them line by line. Never get stuck.

🔥
Daily streak tracking

Keep your streak alive. A small daily habit compounds fast — even five minutes of real conversation is more effective than thirty minutes of flashcards.
